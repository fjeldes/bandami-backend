3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data exclusively for:

  • Providing the Service: Creating and managing your account, authenticating you, processing your submissions through AI models, and delivering evaluations and feedback.
  • Communication: Sending verification emails, password reset links, service announcements, and responding to support inquiries.
  • Billing: Processing payments through Paddle, managing subscriptions and billing.
  • Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve our AI prompts, Service performance, and user experience. We do NOT use your submitted essays or recordings to train AI models unless you explicitly opt in.
  • Security: Rate limiting, fraud prevention, monthly usage tracking, and protecting against unauthorized access.
  • Legal compliance: Meeting applicable legal obligations and responding to lawful requests.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or UK, our legal bases for processing your data are:

  • Contractual necessity: Processing required to provide the Service you requested (account creation, evaluations, payments).
  • Legitimate interests: Service improvement, security, analytics — balanced against your privacy rights.
  • Consent: For optional data processing where you have given explicit consent (e.g., Google OAuth login).
  • Legal obligation: Where required by law.

We may disclose your data if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation. We may also disclose data to protect the rights, property, or safety of Bandami, our users, or the public.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for their marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days.
  • Submitted content (essays, recordings, transcriptions): Retained indefinitely while your account is active to provide you with access to your history. Deleted when you delete your account.
  • Evaluation results (scores, feedback): Stored as part of your exam history. Retained while your account is active.
  • Payment records: Retained for the period required by tax and accounting laws (typically 7 years).
  • Refresh tokens: Hashed and stored for 7 days. Revoked upon logout or password reset.
  • Verification tokens (JWT): Expire after 24 hours. Not stored in database; they are self-contained JWTs.
  • IP addresses (rate limiting): Stored temporarily in memory only. Not persisted to the database.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (salted, one-way) — we never store plain-text passwords.
  • All API communications are encrypted via HTTPS.
  • Database access is restricted to backend services using strong authentication.
  • JWT tokens (access and refresh) are signed with HMAC-SHA256 and have configurable expiration times.
  • Session refresh tokens are stored as SHA-256 hashes, not as plain text.
  • Rate limiting is applied to authentication endpoints to prevent brute force attacks.
  • Google OAuth tokens are never stored; only the Google ID is persisted for account linking.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (account deletion).
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent (e.g., Google OAuth), you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at contacto@bandami.com. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

9. Cookies and Tracking

Bandami does not use cookies for the core Service functionality. Authentication is handled via JWT tokens stored in the client application (e.g., localStorage or secure cookies managed by your frontend application).

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising cookies, or tracking pixels. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and provide notice.

10. Children's Privacy

Bandami is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States (where OpenAI, Google, and Google Cloud servers are located), the United Kingdom (where Paddle servers are located), Chile (where Bandami is based), and the European Union (where Brevo servers are located).

When we transfer data internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or rely on the service provider's certification under applicable data protection frameworks (e.g., EU-US Data Privacy Framework, where applicable).

12. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach involving your personal data, we will notify affected users via email and appropriate authorities within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, as required by applicable law.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or through the Service. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent changes.
